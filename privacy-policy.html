🐝 BuzzMate AI – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 1, 2025

BuzzMate AI is a futuristic beekeeping assistant designed to empower modern apiarists with intelligent forecasting, predictive insights, and field-ready tools.

Your data powers the AI — but it always remains yours.

🔍 What We Collect

  • Email address (for account authentication)
  • Hive records, inspections, and inventory usage
  • Geolocation (only if you grant permission)
  • Weather data for forecasting purposes

🔐 How Your Data is Used

BuzzMate uses your hive and inventory data to power AI-driven forecasts, colony insights, and task reminders.

We do not share your data with third parties. All sensitive data is stored securely in compliance with international standards.

🌍 Data Sovereignty

Your hive data is encrypted and isolated by user ID. Only you — the beekeeper — can access or control it.

🚫 No Ads, No Profiling

BuzzMate AI does not contain ads or tracking mechanisms. We do not build marketing profiles, nor do we sell data.

Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at support@buzzmate.ai. We will process your request within 5 working days. Data deleted is permanent and includes login, hive, inventory, and forecast history.
